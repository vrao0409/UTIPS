Introducing an Interactive Tips Forum

uTips features an interactive and educational interface which lets you share your experiences and learn from others. uTips is also a great place to learn more about how to best prepare for your courses. Through the Tips Forum, you can get specialized advice/information about your classes.

Don't Forget to Check Out the Resources Section

Worried about the price of books, supplies, or any materials you might need for class? Look no further, the Resources section of our webpage features various sellers that you can sort through by class. This way you can try to find any books/class material that you might need being resold at a more affordable price.
